A Comprehensive Platform for Human Transcription Factors and Their Targets.

Introduction


Here, we designed the TFTG database, which aims to provide a large number of available human TF-target gene resources by multiple strategies, besides performing a comprehensive functional and epigenetic annotation and regulatory analysis of TF.

The current version for TFTG catalogs a total of 1218 human TFs and four TF target gene resource categories that were constructed by processing 11,056 TF ChIP-seq datasets, 280 TF perturbation RNA-seq datasets, 805 TF motif profiles and more than 5,000 TF-targets related literatures. In addition, TFTG supports multiple types of TF binding locations on the genome, such as promoter, enhancer, super-enhancer, accessible chromatin and silencer that were identified by processing over 2,000 H3K27ac ChIP-seq and 1,400 ATAC-seq raw data. Especially, TFTG provides four types of TF regulatory analyses for users, including TF gene set enrichment analysis, TF downstream regulatory analysis, Gene upstream regulatory analysis and Pathway enrichment analysis. TFTG provides a user-friendly interface to search, browse, download and visualize information associated with TF regulatory and identification of target genes. We believe that TFTG will become a useful and effective platform for exploring potential functions and regulation of TF in diseases and biological processes.
